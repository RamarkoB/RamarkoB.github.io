Biography

I am currently a senior at the University of Chicago pursuing Bachelor’s Degrees in Computer Science and Economics, and I am incredibly interested in computer science and technology. I was born and raised in the suburbs of Chicago, but over the last three years on campus, I’ve invested all of my free time experimenting and learning about computer science and technology. 

At the University of Chicago, I’ve had the pleasure of working alongside other students who are interested in computer and data science as much as I am. My proudest accomplishments at the University of Chicago are working at the Actuated Experience Lab on campus as a researcher, as well as acting as the President and Chair of the UChicago Open Data Project and the Chief Technology Officer of the largest student-run organization on campus, the Model United Nations of the University of Chicago. From these organizations, I’ve gained so much valuable experience from the organizations I lead to the communities I am a part of. I’m excited to see where I can take on new challenges and more ambitious projects. You can check out my GitHub, contact me at my LinkedIn, or email me if you want to learn more about me, or just want to reach out!
